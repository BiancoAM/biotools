miRNA 3'UTR Mapper

Interactive map of miRNA binding sites on the 3'UTR of any gene. Searches directly in Ensembl and miRDB. Ideal for post-transcriptional analysis and target validation.

Ensembl REST miRDB v5 Interactive SVG Export CSV
✓
Gene symbol search (e.g. ABCB11, TP53)
✓

✓
Prediction score, position, miRNA family
✓
8mer / 7mer / 6mer filters and column sorting
↗

Splicing Analyzer

Predict splicing impact of genetic variants using SpliceAI deep learning scores and MaxEntScan. Dual input: genomic position (hg38) or raw sequence scan. With Ensembl context viewer and clinical interpretation.

SpliceAI (Broad) MaxEntScan Ensembl REST hg38
✓
SpliceAI ΔScores: DS_AG, DS_AL, DS_DG, DS_DL
✓
MaxEntScan 5' donor & 3' acceptor scoring
✓
Genomic sequence context viewer (Ensembl)
✓
Clinical interpretation (pathogenic thresholds)
↗
Links to SpliceAI Lookup, VarSome, OMIM

About me
Molecular Biologist
Geneticist

I work on the study and analysis of genetic variants in both Mendelian (monogenic) disorders and multifactorial diseases, where pathogenesis arises from the interplay between an individual's genetic background and environmental triggers.

My work spans Whole Exome Sequencing (WES) and Whole Genome Sequencing with Long Reads (ONT / PacBio): from DNA extraction and sample preparation, through data analysis with Relational Databases, to the identification and confirmation of variants of interest in rare disease patients. Long-read WGS enables detection of structural variants, repeat expansions and complex rearrangements not resolvable by short-read approaches.

I developed these tools to automate and improve the analysis workflow, making them freely available to the scientific community.

All tools are open source, freely usable for research and education, and welcome community contributions.
