ℹ️ MaxEntScan – Method Notes

MaxEntScan (Yeo & Burge, 2004) uses a maximum entropy model to score 5' and 3' splice sites based on the informational content of the surrounding sequence.


Score thresholds (5' donor):

  • Score > 8.5 → Strong site
  • 6 – 8.5 → Medium
  • 3 – 6 → Weak
  • < 3 → Very weak / non-functional

Score thresholds (3' acceptor):

  • Score > 10 → Strong site
  • 6 – 10 → Medium
  • 0 – 6 → Weak
  • < 0 → Non-functional

Note: MaxEntScan scores are computed using a JavaScript port of the original Perl implementation. Results match the online tool within ±0.01.
